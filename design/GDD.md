Game name: I need more time to cast this spell

Goal: survive until your wizards in the middle cast as pell that kills all enemies. 

Entrance here means enemies come off screen and enter from that side.

First two levels there are one entrance, like in battle of helms deep where you defend against the waves of enemies from 1 direction.

Next three levels there are two entrances where they come from. From various combinations.

Next four levels after are 3 entrances.

Next four levels after are 4 entrances.

Gameplay

In general, the player is WASD moving around and auto-casting everything. The spells they cast kill things in their vicinity like in vampire survivors. You don't level up during gameplay but afterwards. Enemies drop xp gems that you walk over and pick up. The player also can dash with space.

Player health by default is 8. Enemy health by default is 4. First boss is going to have 40 health.

Enemy Behaviour

They generally move towards the wizards in the middle to attack it. But if the hero is within enemy attack range * 1.3 they will target the hero for at least 5 seconds.

20% of enemies are ranged enemies after level 1.

Skill tree

4 main directions

Going up is player power. Faster movement, more player damage, more health, more player projectiles.

Going down is upgrading the towers and barricades, with more health and damage and slow effect and aoe damage. Each level 1 tower is 30% of the player level 1 power slowly increasing up to 100% of the player's damage.

Going left is increasing xp and rewards for beating the level. Chance for enemies to drop health orbs. Drop more xp.

Going right is upgrading the mages in the middle. They can cast spells for you when they reach certain conditions. For example, when enemies get close they can emit a shockwave that pushes and slows enemies. They auto cast their spells when appropriate. Spells that defend themselves and rarely kill things that are close and threatening in case enemies slip through the other defenses, but not for killing the majority of the enemies.
